Comprehensive Testing

We offer a wide range of laboratory tests, ensuring accurate results for early diagnosis and effective treatment planning.
Trust us for reliable, timely, and professional testing services.

FBS & HbA1C (Diabetes)

Essential for diagnosing and managing diabetes, monitoring blood sugar levels, and preventing complications.

CBC, ESR, Urine Test (General Health)

Helps assess overall health, detecting infections, anemia, inflammation, and kidney or urinary issues.

KFT, LFT (Kidney & Liver)

Evaluates kidney and liver health, detecting early signs of diseases or dysfunction.

Thyroid Profile

Measures hormone levels to diagnose thyroid disorders affecting metabolism, energy, and overall health.

Vitamin B12 & D (Vitamins)

Checks for deficiencies that cause fatigue, bone issues, and neurological problems, ensuring optimal health.

Lipid Profile (Heart Care)

Assesses cholesterol and triglyceride levels to determine cardiovascular health and prevent heart disease.

Iron Profile

Evaluates iron levels to identify deficiencies or excess, which can cause anemia and fatigue.

Calcium Test

Checks calcium levels essential for bone health, muscle function, and detecting disorders like osteoporosis.
